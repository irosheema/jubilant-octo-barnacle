THE MANIFESTO

I view digital space not as a static canvas, but as an interactive ecosystem.

My work explores the intersections of real-time generative graphics, feedback loops, and captured moments. Through TouchDesigner networks and photography, I investigate how code can mimic organic structures, how cognitive dissonance reveals itself in human systems, and how digital art can offer spaces for escapism.

Every project is an experiment in capturing motion, texture, and thought.
